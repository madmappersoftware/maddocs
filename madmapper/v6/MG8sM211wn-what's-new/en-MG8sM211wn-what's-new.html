MadMapper Recent Highlights

Meet MadMapper 6

 

Geneva, TBA, 2025

 

MadMapper 6 is now available to buy at Madmapper.com. Users who pre-ordered the v6 upgrade can now download it directly from the Downloads tab in their Account page.

 

This landmark update introduces powerful new tools for creators to experiment with their mapping ideas, perform more intuitively in live contexts, and synchronize entire shows with the brand-new Timeline feature. It’s been one of the most requested additions, and we’re thrilled to finally deliver it to the MAD community.

 

MadMapper’s interface has also received a refined visual overhaul. While everything remains where you expect it, the experience is cleaner, sharper, and more precise. For a quick tour of what’s new, explore the “MadMapper 6 at a Glance” page.

 

To support users worldwide, we’re also launching the new MAD Documentation platform, offering a clearer, more accessible learning experience than ever before. Alongside it, the MAD Certified program is now live on our website, making it easy to find and connect with certified artists, schools, and training partners across the globe.

 

Welcome to the new era of MadMapper!
