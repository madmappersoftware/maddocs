MadMapper Recent Highlights

Meet MadMapper 6

Geneva, TBA, 2025

 

MadMapper 6 is now available to buy at Madmapper.com. People who pre-ordered the v6 upgrade can now download it directly from their your account page in the Downloads section.

 

This landmark update introduces powerful new tools for artists and creatives to develop and experiment with their mapping ideas, perform more intuitively in live contexts, and synchronize entire shows with the brand-new Timeline feature. It’s been one of the community’s most requested additions, and we’re thrilled to finally deliver it to the MAD family.

 

MadMapper’s interface has also received a refined visual overhaul. While everything remains where you expect it, the experience is cleaner, sharper, and more precise. For a quick overview of what’s new, explore the “MadMapper 6 at a Glance” page.

 

To support users worldwide, we’re also launching the new MAD Documentation platform, offering a clearer, more accessible learning experience than ever before. Alongside it, the MAD Certified program is now live on our website, making it easy to find and connect with certified artists, schools, and training partners across the globe.

 

Welcome to the new era of MadMapper!
