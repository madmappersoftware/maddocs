MadMapper, the ultimate mapping software

 

MadMapper is a leading software for video and light mapping used globally by visual artists, scenographers, and so on. Built for creative freedom and live performance, it allows users to map content onto any surface - flat or complex - in real time.

Key features include support for multiple outputs, advanced mesh warping, real-time video playback, audio reactivity, DMX/Art-net lighting control, and recently laser mapping, enabling users to control laser projectors with unprecedented accuracy and flexibility.

It remains highly accessible to newcomers, while offering deep customization for professionals. Therefore MadMapper is widely adopted by art schools, media labs, and training programs around the world, fostering a strong community of learners and creators who shape the future of audiovisual expression.

Video Mapping

 

MadMapper is the reference software for real-time video mapping, combining an intuitive interface with professional technical capabilities.

It enables video and generative content to be projected onto simple or complex surfaces, thanks to advanced warping, Bézier masking or multi-surface management tools. Users can precisely map video onto 2D and 3D volumes, with total control over distortion and content synchronization.

The integrated video engine is ultra-high-performance, capable of handling very high resolutions and playing formats such as HAP, ProRes, standard H264 and more. It supports multiple simultaneous video outputs, making it ideal for immersive installations and large-scale events.

MadMapper also supports live sources via Syphon, Spout, NDI or camera feeds, and offers perfectly fluid playback, whether looped, triggered or timeline-driven.

The software also includes a full-featured GLSL shader editor, enabling the integration of generative effects in real time, and an audio engine that enables video or effects to be driven from sound (spectrum analysis, beat detection, etc.). Compatibility with standard protocols (MIDI, OSC, DMX/Artnet, Ableton Link) makes it a particularly connected and powerful creative platform.

Light Mapping

 

MadMapper gives you precise, creative control over light, thanks to its powerful MadLight feature. The software indeed natively supports DMX, Art-Net, or sACN protocols.

Its integrated LED scanner facilitates spatial calibration of installations, and coupled with video, audio or shaders, light mapping in MadMapper paves the way for dynamic, immersive and totally synchronized light scenography.

MadMapper Key Features

  • Ultra fast Video Player: playback huge resolutions.
  • Multiple projectors: connect as many projectors as your computer can handle.
  • Bezier Masking: handcut, trim and mask your content with advanced bezier geometry.
  • Mesh Warping: precisely fit your content to real surfaces with professional warping tools.
  • Spout / Syphon / NDI: share content with other graphic applications.
  • Live controls: connect any MIDI / OSC controller to MadMapper and perform in realtime.
  • Material Editor: use the MadMapper shader editor to create your generative texture and share them with the community.
  • 3D Calibration: match your 3d model to the real world.
  • Space Scanner: use your camera to capture the projector’s point of view.
  • Built-in generative content: colors, patterns, grids, text, and generative shaders natively included.

MadLaser

 

MadLaser is an extension of MadMapper dedicated to laser mapping, offering precise and creative control of laser projectors.

Compatible with multiple Laser hardware interfaces, MadLaser transforms any shape, text or generative content into laser trajectories in real time, and more, in true MadMapper fashion.

Create with ease fluid, sharp and expressive animations, directly synchronized with video, audio or lighting.

Accessible and powerful, the recent MadLaser extension opens up a new dimension in audiovisual creation.

MadLaser Key Features

  • Unlimited laser outputs: as many as your computer can handle.
  • Multi Hardware supported: Etherdream, ShowNET, Helios, FB3, FB4 (with Pangolin and Windows), AVB/Dante, LaserCube, IDN.
  • Laser bezier lines: ultra precise vector graphics.
  • 3D object rendering from .OBJ format.
  • Realtime video vectorization hardware accelerated image analysis and processing.
  • Ilda files import / export.
  • Vector Materials: realtime shader based.
  • Up to 100 kpps scanners: high definition graphics.
  • Laser Soft Edge on laser quads.
  • Laser Dispatch: spread complex content over multiple lasers.

MadProjectorControl

 

The MadProjectorControl extension uses the PJLink protocol to remotely control any number of compatible video projectors.

Totally cueable, this extension allows to schedule and automate power and shutter activity.

 

The features needed by the pros:

  • Compatible with any PJLink compliant device.
  • Control any number of projectors.
  • Cuable, schedulable and automatable.
  • Allows power on/off and shutter remotely.
  • Displays device information.

MiniMad

 

MiniMad is an hardware plug-and-play device designed to playback your MadMapper setups with automatic network synchronization for multi-projection or DMX / Artnet output to control lighting and LED equipment.

 

MiniMad makes your installation easy and affordable: it hooks up to a projector and plays movie files on surfaces exported from MadMapper. Put your expensive and bulky computers out of a job.

Small by size but feature rich: with the tiny MiniMad, you export quad, triangle circle or mask surfaces as well as their content from a selected projector

in MadMapper. Or you can switch the MiniMad to lighting mode and output DMX/Artnet channel data to your light fixtures.

Discreet and Autonomous: the MiniMad can run on its own for a very long time: we have one unit in the lab running for more than a year! In case of a power failure, the MiniMad will automatically restart without any user intervention. All the mapping modifications you do are automatically saved on the SD-Card.

Team Power: multiple MiniMads can be synchronized with each other over ethernet to make a cluster of synchronized video players. Zero configuration needed! MadMapper will automatically split movies and optimize files for playback.

 

MiniMad Main Features

  • Automatic Synchronisation of video media playback when multiple MiniMads are connected to the same ethernet network.
  • In-Depth Quality Export with precise video controls.
  • Up to 64 universes for ArtNet/DMX.
  • Autonomous playback of content recorded from MadMapper.
  • MultiChannel Audio when using Light mode.
  • Native Video Playback up to 1080p30 in optimal conditions.
  • Physical controls with friendly buttons to tweak your mapping directly on location.
  • Video or Light. 2 playback modes available.
  • VideoWall easy setup one click full automated process.
  • Zero configuration, as simple as that.
  • Playback modes: loop, random, list... configure your MiniMad to playback your videos the way you want it.
  • Mesh Warping and Perspective Pinning with adjustable manual controls.
  • Remote control your MiniMads directly from MadMapper.

Technical Specifications

  • Raspberry Pi 2
  • 7 buttons control panel
  • 1x HDMI output up to 1080p (1920x1080 at 60Hz)
  • Size 9 x 6 x 3 cm (approx. 3.5 x 2.3 x 1.2 inches)
  • MiniMad is setup for either video mapping or light mapping; it cannot do both at the same time, otherwise you would need two different units.
  • Media playback (video) is automatically synced when MiniMad units are connected to the same ethernet network but it can induce a one-frame delay between the different units, therefore the device is not suitable for blending/soft-edge.
  • For MiniMads that will be running continuously for months, for example for an installation, we recommend using industrial-grade Micro SD cards + having backup SD cards with your project already exported.
